Meet the presidents


Dr. Isaac Ojo Fayomi

Dr. Isaac Ojo Sunday Fayomi is an internationally celebrated scholar, inspirational speaker, author, and teacher. He is an established proven academia and global researcher in the area of mechanical metallurgy. He was born in Iyara, Kogi State, Nigeria, on March 14th, 1984. He has a combination of multifaceted experience and qualification around engineering discipline. He obtained National Diploma (Distinction ) from Federal Polytechnic Birnin Kebbi, Kebbi State, Nigeria, Higher National Diploma in (Metallurgical and Materials Engineering) Federal Polytechnic Idah, Kogi State with (Upper credit), Bachelor’s of Engineering, (Honours) in (Mechanical Engineering) from Bells University of Technology (2nd Class upper). He bagged his Master’s and Doctoral degree in Metallurgical Engineering with Distinction (Cum Laude) from the Department of Chemical, Metallurgical and Materials Engineering, Tshwane University of Technology, Pretoria, South Africa. He has a postgraduate diploma in environmental and natural resource management from Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ago-Iwoye. Fayomi has been instructing in the University and research environment since 2007.

Ojo Sunday Fayomi began his career immediately after National Youth Service Corp in Abia state at Covenant University as a cleaner in September 2007. He rose to be a distinguished exceptional researcher to various international institutions. Ojo Sunday Fayomi today has contributed immensely to the advancement of materials world as a visiting scholar to several universities locally and internationally. Ojo Sunday Fayomi who is a visiting research director at Surface Engineering Research Centre, Tshwane University of Technology, Pretoria, South Africa and Surface Engineering.

He has to his credit over (350) indexed international and local peer-reviewed journals with strong impact factors, broad readership, and citations. Furthermore, Fayomi is an External Examiner to several Nigerian universities at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels, and some foreign universities, among others, is the University of Johannesburg in South Africa; He has supervised over150 research work both at the postgraduate and undergraduate level.

Fayomi is a sought after inspirational guest speaker and lecturer to many industries, universities, and institutes. Fayomi is known to be a reviewer of Scientific Journal and Conference in the related field; examiner for National Research Foundation, South Africa. He is a member of several engineering bodies such as Council for the Regulation of Engineering in Nigerian (COREN), Member Nigeria Society of Engineers (NSE), Registered Academic Member Water Institute of Southern Africa (AMWISA), Member Nigeria Metallurgical Society (NMS), and Member Nigeria Association of Technologist in Engineering.

He is an achieved researcher and lecturer who has harvested several awards internationally, among others, is the Scientific Achievement by South African Association for Advancement in Science Medal Award (A2S3 Medal for Original Research). Fayomi is a Postdoc Research Scholar and an Awardee of the Institutional Best Postdoctoral fellow for Teaching and Innovations at Tshwane University of Technology 2017. He also emerged as the best doctoral degree award student of 2015 at Tshwane University of Technology. Awardee for He is well-reputed nationally and internationally in the areas of Mechanical Materials Mechanics. He was named the Outstanding African Researcher, 2019, and the Most Innovative Scholar in Composite deposition. Also, according to 2017, 2018, 2019 rating of researchers globally by Elsevier Scopus (Scival), Fayomi is ranked the number one (1) researcher in the world in the area of Composite, Electrodeposition of Materials. Ranked the number one (1) researcher in Nigeria 2019 in the field of Materials Science and Engineering, he is ranked number 3 top published author in Nigerian in accredited Journal by Elsevier Scopus. He has a patent to his credit with the South Africa Ministry of Education and Cooperates affair.

Dr Isaac Fayomi is a passionate, purpose-driven, self-motivated, and result-oriented natural leader. He is a multi-talented person, perfectionist, and president of Ignite World- an organization founded by him for helping the needy, youth, and family to discover self. Fayomi is the founder of a monthly publication called DISCOVERY, which has a network of vibrant conference and inspiration presentations. He has published many weekly motivational and transformational materials by dint of hard work, commitment, and dedication to the vision. Dr. Isaac Fayomi has trained hundreds to thousands of people through ignite world motivational summit, seminars, workshops and conferences. He presently serves as life coach and mentor to several people in Nigeria and around the world. As an international conference speaker, he has spoken to audiences in Belgium, Mauritius, United States, Grease, United Kingdom, South Africa, and Nigeria

Today, Isaac Fayomi is a multi-gifted inspirational speaker who addresses critical issues that affect every aspect of human concept, professional practices, social, and spiritual empowerment. Fayomi is happily married to his sweetheart Gloria, and they are blessed with two children Isaac Jnr and Triumph.

Gloria Fayomi

Gloria Fayomi is the wife of the president of Ignite world international-Dr. Isaac Fayomi. She is the co-founder of Ignite discovery a monthly bulletins that go out monthly in thousands to different part of the world. She is an international speaker with passion to bring hope to the hopeless. This vision has prompt her to establish a foundation called “INSPIRED” that reach out to the youth, widows, orphans so as to bring life to the lifeless and get them empowered.

On her monthly online seminar, workshop and conference video broadcast, INSPIRED with Gloria Fayomi, she shares practical insight with simplicity on various life critical issues that affect every aspect of human concept, professional practices, social, and spiritual empowerment which ignite world is commissioned. She has authored and co-author several books and bulletins with her husband.

Despite several engagement and schedules, Gloria is known as an addict learner like her husband. She holds a master degree in Environment Management from Lagos State University and currently rounding up her doctoral degree in Geography from University of South Africa. Currently she heads a newly mandated motivational concept from Ignite world Called “CATCH FIRE”.

Gloria Fayomi is married to her priceless heart Isaac, and they are blessed with two lovely children, Isaac Chinanza Fayomi Junior and Triumph Chukwuka, Fayomi
