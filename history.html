History


I have been seeing strong hands of God in my life but between 2011 to 2015 during my master and doctoral degree while in a foreign land, I saw His strange hands. God gave me unusual speed, special rest and strange access to him. I was on top of the world in my academic pursuit knowing fully well how a young man with poor background will soon become a Doctor of Technology in Metallurgical Engineering. Not only that, between a sudden incredible, unbelievable and unthinkable moment, I could see a change of level with global recognition and decoration I was harvesting left and right through God. I know the value of this and I couldn’t hide this for anything. My life has change and God gave me a new beginning. Knowing all this, I resolve to thank him for his special acts upon me for 21 day prayer and fasting to seal heavens and forging forward.

Suddenly

While thanking God for great deed of staying in foreign land and thinking of enjoying enviable academics world thereafter, I fell asleep. In the early hour of the first night of waiting on the Lord for 21 days prayer and fasting, God took me on a trance, in that I was on the top of the mountain where I could see all that man travail upon. I couldn’t clearly understand the interpretation and as I was asking what was all this, I woke up. On the third day, I saw again the same vivid pictures. This deep revelation kept on coming every three day interval and until the last twenty one day, this time clearer. God show me two specific people “one groaning and the other grumbling”. The one groaning were battered, dejected, rejected, afflicted, naked and inflicted. The one grumbling are working harder and nothing to show for it, they were lifeless, hopeless and helpless. Suddenly, I saw the handwriting on the wall inscribed with golden pen written ACT 7:34. Then God said son, this is your new assignment…

“I have seen the travail of men as they groan and grumb; now, I have given you the keys of the kingdom to triumphantly bring hope to the hopeless, help to the helpless, life to the lifeless and the battered should begin to bear. Hence, groom me “special” people of greatness and raise me stars to ignite their world through the ignite word”.

Today, with tirelessly commitment to vision, Ignite word international has birth Ignite discovery bulletins, Ignite catch fire, Ignite crystal clear, Ignite clear cut.

You are specially welcome to Ignite world, where everyone is “SPECIAL”


Yours in Christ, Isaac & Gloria Fayomi
